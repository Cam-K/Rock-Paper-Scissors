How to play Rock, Paper, Scissors

In the game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, you get to get to choose either rock, paper, scissors. The computer will pick one too. Each of the 3 can win or lose to each either. Rock beats Scissors, but loses to paper. Paper beats rock but loses to scissors. Scissors beats paper but loses to rock. These are the basic rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors. There are 4 game modes. Standard is when you play until you nolonger have any lives left. 2/3 is a Rock, Paper, Scissors game out of 2/3. 3/5 is a Rock, Paper, Scissors game out of 3/5. Free play is a game going as long as you would like to play. There are no lives in this game mode.
